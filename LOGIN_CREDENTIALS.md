## How Notifications Work

1. **Click the bell icon** in the top-right corner
2. **See unread count** displayed on the bell badge
3. **Click any notification** to:
   - Automatically mark it as read
   - Navigate to the relevant section
   - Close the notification panel

You can also:
- Click "Mark all read" to mark all notifications as read
- Click the X button to delete individual notifications
- Click "Mark as read" on individual notifications without navigating
